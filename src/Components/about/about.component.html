About

Hi, I'm Emirhan Hasırcı, a dedicated third-year Computer Engineering student with a profound passion for software technologies since 2018. Currently pursuing my degree at Bartın University, I actively cultivate my skills in full-stack and indie game development during my free time. In my recent role as the Founder Lead of Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) Bartın, I established and led a community dedicated to Google developer technologies. This experience enhanced my collaborative and leadership abilities, providing a platform for knowledge exchange among aspiring professionals. Simultaneously, I am a Scholar at the Game and Application Academy, where I am immersed in the vibrant realms of game development and application design. This opportunity provides me with invaluable insights and hands-on experience in these thriving fields.
